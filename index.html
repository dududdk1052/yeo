The Artist

Yeo Yeoung-A —
A world painted
with warmth

Born in Korea, now rooted in the gentle light of Dublin, I paint the emotions that don't fit into words. My work lives in the space between memory and the present moment.

My studio in Dublin is both a home and a haven — a place where students discover their own visual voice, and where I continue to chase mine.
